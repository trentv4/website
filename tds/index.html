Top-down Shooter

This is one of my longer projects. Several years in the making, but with only a few days in the programming and design of. It's a top down shooter, where you play as a Navy SEAL attempting recapture of the captain of the commercial barge, the USS Hermangildo. To aid you in this, you have been provided helpful medical stims and a variety of weapons to suit your preferences. Good luck!

Solo project with creative input from Erlend Amdal.
