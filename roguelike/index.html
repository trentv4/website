Everwood Dungeon

This is one of my favorite memories programming. I've spent maybe 2, 3 weeks of work time to bring it to this point, most of it on vacation around summer 2015. I haven't put much actual content into it, but the gameplay elements are there and sound. This also shows my magnificent artistic skills, which I may or may not come back to fix.

This project is heavily inspired from classic roguelikes, notable Nethack and POWDER (both of which are my favorites.) It's a simple dungeon-crawling game, where you experience different enemies based on the level. Hopefully you can upgrade your equipment before facing the deadly knights deeper down!

Solo project. Lots of inspiration taken from POWDER

Game Controls
